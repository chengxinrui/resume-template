\textbf{Core Research (First Author)}
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf {Physics-Constrained Unsupervised Neural Operator for Long-Horizon PDE Learning on Generalized Geometries (\textbf{First Author}, IJCAI CCF-A)}
    \begin{itemize}[label=\tiny$\bullet$, leftmargin=1em]
        \item \textbf{Pain Points:} Existing unsupervised neural operators rely on differential residuals for physical constraints. High-order spatial derivatives often cause numerical instability and error accumulation in unstructured geometries and long-horizon autoregressive rollouts.
        \item \textbf{Core Innovations:} Proposed the \textbf{Integral-Consistent Physics Embedding (LIPE)} to reconstruct PDE constraints from differential residuals to integral evolution forms, significantly improving training stability and reducing VRAM overhead without labels. Built a Geometry-Agnostic Latent Adaptation (GALA) to unify unstructured geometric representations and a Gated Spectral Evolution Operator (GSEO) to preserve high-frequency structures, achieving stable long-term predictions in complex geometries.
        \item \textbf{Empirical Results:} Reduced 20-step rollout error to \textbf{0.09\%} in 1D high-advection scenarios, maintaining stability in $T=50$ extrapolation; achieved \textbf{3.02\%} 20-step error in 2D Navier–Stokes; achieved \textbf{4.06\%} drag coefficient error in 3D semi-supervised geometry tasks, significantly outperforming baselines.
    \end{itemize}
    \item \textbf {Integral-Consistent Basis-Universal Neural Operators for Diverse PDE Classes (\textbf{First Author}, NeurIPS CCF-A, Under Review)}
    \begin{itemize}[label=\tiny$\bullet$, leftmargin=1em]
        \item \textbf{Pain Points:} Traditional spectral neural operators bind interaction weights to fixed Fourier frequency structures, making it difficult to share dynamic representations across different PDE classes (elliptic/parabolic/hyperbolic) and causing error drift in long-term predictions.
        \item \textbf{Core Innovations:} Proposed a \textbf{Basis-Universal Evolution Framework} that decouples spectral basis representations from interaction weight matrices. Constructed a unified complex latent space interaction mechanism independent of frequency indices, enabling shared dynamics modeling across PDE classes. Introduced geometry-aware latent modulation and integral-consistent constraints to enhance generalization and long-term stability under complex geometries.
        \item \textbf{Empirical Results:} Maintained 20-step rollout error at the \textbf{0.09\%} level in 1D advection-diffusion tasks during cross-PDE class validation; achieved \textbf{1.90\%} 20-step error in 2D Navier–Stokes forcing scenarios; reduced $C_d$ error to \textbf{3.95\%} in 3D semi-supervised geometry tasks, outperforming spectral and integral baselines.
    \end{itemize}
\end{itemize}
